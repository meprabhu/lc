GO BACK TO WEEKLY PROGRESS

Synchronous Session:
Throughout this session, we learned about the things to watch out for during a conversation. 1. Don't multitask. 2. Don't pontificate. 3. Use open-ended questions. 4. Go with the flow. 5. If you don't know, say that you don't know. 6. Don't equate your experience with theirs. 7. Try not to repeat yourself. 8. Stay out of the weeds. 9. Listen. 10. Be brief.

Asynchronous Session:
We were instructed to make a list of things we should avoid in order to have a wonderful conversation in this session. Not paying attention. interfering with the speaker. Assuming you understand the message before the other person finishes. Message misinterpretation. Keeping unpleasant topics at bay. 6. Reacting rather than replying 7. Failure to retain an open mind. 8. Being deceptive. 9. Attacking a person's character rather than their actions. 10. Allowing your emotions to control your reaction.
